The Charity of Saint Martin of Tours
Martin of Tours was a Roman soldier who cut his cloak in half to save the life of a freezing beggar. Since half of everything he owned belong to the Roman Empire, he gave his own half. That night Martin had a vision of Jesus wearing half of the cloak. When he awoke, his cloak was restored.
