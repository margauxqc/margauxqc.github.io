About Margaux

I'm an illustrator and graphic designer living in London with my lovely wife. I'm German-American and grew up in Maryland, but I've spent time in various other places: most recently Oakland, CA.
My work focuses a lot on editorial and children's book work, and I'm also passionate about design and the web. My favorite way to work is using a mix of traditional and digital media.

I'm available for freelance work and art licensing!
