About Margaux

I am a German-American illustrator from Maryland. I currently live in a green area of London with my lovely wife.

I received my MFA Illustration at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco, and a BA(Hons) in graphic design from Central Saint Martins in London.

My current focus is on editorial illustration. However, I am also passionate about design and the web and like to create small animations. My favorite way to work is digital painting, but I also like to mix digital and traditional media. When using traditional media I especially love drawing and painting with colored ink.

To get in touch for freelance work and art licensing, please send me a message!
