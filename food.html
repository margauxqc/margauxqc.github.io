Personal Project: Küchen Klatsch Website
Coding and designing this website was a great learning experience. I emphasized progressive enhancement and progressive web design, but I also got to have lots of fun experimenting with CSS! The website is a showcase for some (mostly) family recipes.
Getting the chance to complete some food illustrations was the major motivation behind this project.
The name is a reference to "Kaffee Klatsch", aka. the important German ritual of coffee & cake & gossip.
